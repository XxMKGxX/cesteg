Welding

Argon welding of carbon steel and stainless steel products. - Stick welding of carbon steel and stainless steel products. - Argon and stick welding of carbon and stainless steel pipes. - Welding of structural stainless steel and carbon steel products. - Welding of carbon steel and stainless tanks. - Design of weld procedures. - All coded welding works. - Rebuilding of warn parts and Hard facing. - Training of welders and pipe fitters

Industrial Pipework

Fabricating and installation of carbon and stainless steel pipes. - Fabrication of carbon steel and stainless steel tanks as per specification, - Fabrication of threaded steel pipes and all PVC pipes. - Identifying test circuits and spading limits for flushing and hydrotesting of pressure vessels, - Erection of tanks and high pressure vessels, - Replacement of warn out pipes, - Maintenance and shut down works, - Water and sewer construction /upgrading, - High pressure welding and all coded works. - Commissioning of various Plants.

High Voltage Electrical Installation

Our company does not limit itself to Low Voltage (LV) installations and is able to offer its clients' a full design and installation of HV/LV systems, both temporary and permanent. We do Maintenance of Substations, Transformers and HT switching.

Industrial Pumps

Installations, maintenance and overhaul of Warman slurry pumps, hose pumps, reciprocation pumps, gear pumps, vane pumps, vacuum pumps, and centrifugal pumps. Pump alignment. This includes trouble shooting during commissioning and hand over.

Commercial Installation and Maintenace

Commercial and Industrial installations form the backbone of the company’s day to day operations. Our commercial side covers all manners of works including factory and office installations, amenity‐lighting and even town center and private clients. Fusan Construction carries an extensive stock of site temporary distribution panels, 400v/230v. Our temporary site mains panels can accept loads of up to 3,000amps on a three phase load distribution, and all are supplied with earth leakage protection. We are able to respond urgently to any given emergency, or alternatively plan and install a long term solution to an on-going project.
